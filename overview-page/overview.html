Objects

These are the objects that I found with five different forms of documentation. I documentated the objects on their color pallete, length, a sketch drawing, a short description, and the location that I found them (which is found on the home page)


A colorful, slightly bent Miller Light bottle cap.

A worn-down chapstick with the outer packaging ripped off.

Two rusty silver rings I found intertwined together.

A brown, decaying banana peel I found.

An empty pack of cigarettes with a camel on the front.

A used mint green dental floss pick.

A bulldog team mascot small eraser.

A worn, dark blue lighter with no fluid left in it.

A green thumbtack with dirt on the needle.

A silver tiny bell keychain that doesn’t ring.

Pamphlet

This is a zine/pamphlet I made documenting all ten objects I found. I wanted a way to display all the information I collected in one place, so I arranged each object's sketch, color palette, measurements, and description into a small booklet format. It was a way to make the data feel more physical and visually interesting rather than just a list of facts.
